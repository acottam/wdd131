Site Name

Name: Pacific Northwest Family Adventures

Tagline: A curated guide to kid-friendly hikes, museums, and hidden gems across Oregon and Washington.

Site Purpose

I chose this subject because exploring the Pacific Northwest with my family is one of my favorite pastimes. This project lets me combine local adventures with web development skillsâ€”responsive design, JS interactivity, and a polished, accessible presentation.

Scenarios

  1. As a parent with two kids under 10, I want easy hikes with restrooms and picnic areas so we can plan a half-day trip without meltdowns.
  2. As a first-time visitor to Seattle, I want kid-friendly museums near public transit so the day stays simple.
  3. As a weekend warrior, I want to check trail conditions and a 48-hour forecast for a hidden gem within 90 minutes of Portland.
